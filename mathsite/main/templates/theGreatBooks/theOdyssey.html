Notes From The Odyssey

Below is a table to keep track of all the significant characters that I come across in The Odyssey:

Character Name About
Ulysses Also known as Odysseus, King of Ithaca, and son of Laertes and Anticleia. Father of Telemachus and hudband of Penelope. Ulysses is known as the hero of The Odyssey.
Telemachus Son of Ulysses and Penelope, also searches for his father in The Odyssey.
Hyperion The sun god, also known as Helios.
Calypso Daughter of the Titan Atlas. She's a nymph of the mythical island of Ogygia.
Neptune Roman god of the sea, and controller of winds and storms. Son of Saturn and Ops.
Aegisthus Son of Thestes and Pelopia and lover of Clytemnestra.
Agamemnon Son of Atreus and king of Achaean army.
Orestes Son of Agamemnon, and King of Mycenae (also known as Argos).
Mercury God of translators and interpreters, and the most clever of the Olympian gods.
